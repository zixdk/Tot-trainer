Jin'rokh Important Mechanics

Lightning Bolt (Focused Lightning)
Move OUT of the Pool and Explode at the Corner!!!
Ionization
Use all your Immunities (Ice Block, Shadow Cloak, Deterrence, Bubble). Symbiosis Assignments: R-Druid Symbiosis Mage (for Ice Block) / Boomkin Symbiosis Rogue (for Cloak of Shadows) for Extra Immunities!!! If you don't have Immunity: Move OUT of pool and Dispel yourself. Remember to use Def CDs before Dispel!
IMPORTANT
Never Explode Bolt IN POOL!!!! Never Explode/Dispel Ionization IN the pool!!!!

Horridon Important Mechanics

Door 1 (Farraki)
Dodge Sand Traps (Never stand in). Healers ALWAYS Dispel Blazing Debuff!!!
Door 2 (Gurubashi)
Focus Venom Mobs + Interrupt Casts (Prio always Green Mobs!!!). Dodge Venom pools (Color Green). Healers: If we have cast Debuff, 24/7 Dispel Venom poisons!!!!
Door 3 (Drakkari)
RDPS + Healer Team: Group up at Specified Position. MDPS Always with Tank!!!
Kill 3 BIG Mobs and Dodge Frost Orbs (they Hit like a Truck). Important: Healers 24/7 Keep Dispelling!!!!
Door 4 (Amani)
Kill 3 Bear + Kill 3 Big Shaman. MDPS Never Stand in Front of Bears (They HIT Frontals and you GET One shot!!!).
RDPS + Healers GROUP UP at Specific Position!!!! Dodge Totems on the Ground.
Main Mechanic 1: Charge
If you have Charge MOVE away from Team!!! Never IGNORE Charge. Ignore Charge = 3-4 PLAYERS Dead + Ban Loot!!!
Main Mechanic 2: Dire Fixation
When Boss Active, sometimes Players GET Debuff Called: Dire FIXATION.
That IS a Dino which only you can see. Sometimes you need to HIT HIM. If you don't hit him he will reach you and kill you!!!!
